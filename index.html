Travel to Iceland

Why iceland

  • Aurora

    • The view is stunning if you're lucky to see it. we visited toward the end of winter. Majority of the day is in total darkness. We are extremly lucky to catch the aurora on our first night.

  • Waterfalls

    • Be ready for the amazing journey to all the waterfalls. We joined a tour group to see all the waterfalls in the golden circle.

    Each waterfall is unique to its landscape. We were able to take some hikes around the waterfalls. My favorite waterfall is the skogafoss where you can stand right next to it and feel the grandois of all the water vapor falling on your face.

  • The Hot Springs

    • There are multiple hot springs in Iceland. We visited the Blue Lagoon and Sky Lagoon. The blue Lagoon is filled with mostly tourists and is fully packed when we visited. Our Airbnb host recommended Sky Lagoon. We are extremly happy that we are able to fit it in our schedule. We went on our last day in Iceland and absolutely LOVED it.
